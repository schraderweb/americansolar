    REFERRAL PROGRAM

    Refer a Friend. They Switch to Solar.Earn Extra Money

    You can make money each time you refer someone to us when they successfully switch to solar. Through our referral program, you can help your family and friends save money, increase their property value and take control of their energy costs while playing their part in reducing environmental impact.

    Ready to help your family and friends save up to 50% or more on their electricity bills and earn money while doing it?

    Fill out the required information below to submit your referral.

    Earn $500 for every referral who signs up solar with us.

    You do not have to be our customer to become part of our solar referral program.

    There is no limit on how many people you can refer to us.

    You will receive your referral fee within 2 weeks once the solar system to be
    installed at the given address passes city inspection.

    To be eligible, the referral you submit must be expecting our call regarding a new
    solar system for their home. They must be a US resident, 18 years of age or older.
    They must own their own home and their electric bill must be in their name. Your
    referral must not be from the same household as you are and you cannot refer
    yourself.

    Solar system size must be a minimum of a 4kW system. The average solar system
    size is a 5kW.
